Smart monitoring system for post-oncology surgery patients. Analyzes real-time status to detect worrying symptoms early and ensures direct clinic connection, enhancing clinical safety.

An intelligent assistant for doctors that automatically fills visit data into electronic medical records by analyzing consultation context, cutting down administrative cognitive load.

System for modeling and simulating workflows. Uses applied statistics to predict bottlenecks and proposes the most effective pathways for resource allocation.
