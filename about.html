Who we are

Abba Group MB is a joint project of a physician and an engineer. We combine clinical expertise and modern AI technologies to simplify every visit and every entry in the medical system.

Our approach is not "another loud platform," but quiet, precise tools that integrate into the physician's existing workflows.

We understand that physicians need solutions that work seamlessly in their daily practice, not disruptive systems that require extensive training or workflow changes. Our tools are designed to be invisible helpers that reduce administrative burden and let healthcare professionals focus on what matters most: patient care.

Focus: cognitive amplifiers

We create "cognitive amplifiers" — tools that enhance a physician's ability to work efficiently without adding complexity to their routine.

  • Reducing administrative burden on physicians
  • Automating prescription and referral generation
  • Minimum clicks, maximum value
  • Integration into familiar browser-based workstations
  • Seamless voice-to-data conversion
  • Privacy-first approach to patient data
